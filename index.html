Some of the Programs

Initiatives that Transform Lives

At Tell All, we offer a variety of programs designed to promote the education and empowerment of girls in underserved communities.

Education for Girls

We provide educational support for girls in underserved communities, promoting school enrollment and retention. Our program includes providing educational materials, tutoring, and scholarships to ensure that every girl has access to a quality education and can reach her full potential.

Literacy and Development

We work to improve literacy among girls and young women by providing reading lessons and resources that help strengthen their skills. Our goal is to promote literacy and ensure that every participant can read and write with confidence, facilitating access to more opportunities in the future.

Counseling and Support

We provide psychological support and counseling for girls who have been victims of abuse or trauma. Our team of trained professionals offer therapy and coaching sessions, helping them overcome challenges and build a healthier, more positive life.
